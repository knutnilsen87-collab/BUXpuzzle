# Privacy Policy Draft

BUXPuzzle does not currently require account login, location access, contacts, camera, microphone, or tracking permission.

The current build stores local gameplay progress on the device using Unity PlayerPrefs.

If analytics, ads, cloud saves, crash reporting, or in-app purchases are added later, this policy must be updated before release.

## Data Currently Expected

- Local progress values such as XP, tokens, fragments, and current level.
- Local settings or gameplay state required for play.

## Data Not Currently Expected

- Personal identity data.
- Precise location.
- Contact information.
- User-generated content.
- Third-party advertising identifiers.

## Contact

Support URL and contact email must be added before store submission.
